# Tennis Serve: Video Resources & Coaching Breakdowns

## Top YouTube Channels

### 1. Essential Tennis (Ian Westermann)
https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCeCmnibUIaXUI1WEbUO7lVw
Methodical, concept-first instruction. One of the oldest and most trusted channels. Strong for intermediate players who want the "why" behind each cue.

### 2. Intuitive Tennis (Nikola Aracic)
https://www.youtube.com/@IntuitiveTennis
Feeling-based and kinesthetic. Serves playlist covers toss, takeback, pronation, leg drive, flat, kick, slice. Best for players getting worse by overthinking.

### 3. Top Tennis Training (Simon Konov)
https://www.youtube.com/c/TopTennisTrainingNet
Drill-heavy, progression-oriented. Physical training content (core rotation, explosive power) complements technique work. Simon's own serve transformation is a compelling case study.

### 4. Crunch Time Coaching (Peter Freemantle)
https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCwDXnQWw5E83CF1Dai79M8A
ATP tour analysis-focused. Frame-by-frame elite serve breakdowns. 2024 Hurkacz analysis is standout. Free 7-Day Serve Challenge available.

### 5. Meike Babel Tennis
Former WTA professional. Strong on fixing specific errors (waiter's tray, racket drop). One of few female tour professionals producing coaching content.

### 6. TPA Tennis (Tom Allsopp + Jeff Salzenstein)
https://www.youtube.com/@TomAllsopp
Biomechanics-forward. Clearest free kinetic chain explanations on YouTube. Frequent collaboration with ATP-pedigreed Jeff Salzenstein.

### 7. Tom Avery Tennis / CTW Academy
Strong on serve types taught separately. Free serve course at ctwacademy.com.

## Recommended Viewing Order

### Stage 1 — Foundations (Week 1)
1. "How To Hit The Perfect Tennis Serve In 5 Simple Steps" — motion sequence
2. "The 5 Most Common Serve Problems & Fixes" — know pitfalls early
3. "What Is The BEST Serve Stance?" — pick stance and commit

### Stage 2 — Understanding Power (Week 2)
4. "Tennis Trophy Position & Correct Loading Explained" — load before unload
5. "Master the Serve Kinetic Chain ft. Jeff Salzenstein" — the energy chain
6. "Perfect Serve LEG DRIVE Technique" — most underdeveloped area

### Stage 3 — Spin Variations (Weeks 3-4)
7. "Tennis Serve Pronation Explained For Flat, Slice & Topspin" — foundational for all spin
8. "The Flat, Slice & Kick Tennis Serve Lesson" — apply pronation to each type

### Stage 4 — Elite Reference & Practice
9. "ATP Serve Slow Motion Compilation 2020" — watch with fresh eyes
10. "Roger Federer Slow Motion Serve Analysis" — detailed commentary
11. "30 Minute Practice Routine" — structured practice framework

### Stage 5 — Physical Preparation (Ongoing)
12. "Shoulder Warm Up for Tennis Serve" — before every session
13. "Shoulder & Rotator Cuff Exercises" — 3x weekly maintenance

**If stuck/plateaued:** Serve Unlocked by Tomaz Mencinger is the recommended paid reset.
